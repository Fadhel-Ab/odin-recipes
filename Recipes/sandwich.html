Grilled cheese sandwich Recipe

Home

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a classic comfort food made with melted cheese pressed between two slices of buttery, golden-brown toasted bread.
Crisp on the outside and gooey on the inside, it combines rich, savory flavors with a satisfying crunch, making it a timeless favorite for all ages.
Perfect on its own or paired with a warm bowl of soup!

Ingredients

  • Bread (2 slices, any type you prefer, like white, whole wheat, or sourdough)
  • Butter or margarine (for spreading on the bread)
  • Cheese (1-2 slices or shredded, such as cheddar, mozzarella, or American cheese)

    • optional

  • Garlic powder or herbs (for extra flavor)
  • Tomato slices, bacon, or ham (for a more filling sandwich)

Steps

  1. Start by buttering one side of two slices of bread.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat, and place one slice, buttered side down, in the pan.
  3. Add a layer of your favorite cheese—cheddar, mozzarella, or a mix—and top with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until the bread is golden and crispy, and the cheese is melted. Serve hot and enjoy!
